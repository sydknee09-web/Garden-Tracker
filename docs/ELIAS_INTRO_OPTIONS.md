# Elias Intro — 15 Alternative Scripts

**Purpose:** Pick your favorite. Each option is a complete 5-beat intro + closing. Mix and match beats from different options if you prefer.

| Beat | Dialogue |
|------|----------|
| 1 | Welcome, Traveler. I'm glad you found your way here. |
| 2 | You can call me Elias. I'll take you up the mountain and help you find the peace between the peaks. |
| 3 | In this Sanctuary, we name those weights. We pack them into your Satchel as Stones. They are the peaks you choose to climb. |
| 4 | But a mountain is moved one pebble at a time. Strike a stone with your Hammer to find its pebbles. |

## Beat 3 Alternatives — 10 Options (Satchel)

**The metaphor:** You're climbing a mountain. Stones block the path. You break them, carry them in your Satchel to the Sanctuary, and burn them in the Hearth to clear the path and feed the fire. Beat 3 must bridge mountains → stones → satchel → fire.
