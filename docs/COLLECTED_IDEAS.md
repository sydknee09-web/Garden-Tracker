# Collected Ideas

## Fire & Stones

- **Fire size scaling:** When dropping stones in, we can increase the size of our fire by 10% up to 3 times so it looks bigger.

## Ambient Effects (Time of Day)

- **Morning:** Add steam movement off coffee cup.
- **Night:** Add beer frothing to beer cup.
- **General:** Smoke tendrils, twinkling lights, or similar ambient effects.
