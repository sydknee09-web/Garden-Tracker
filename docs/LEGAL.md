## How to Host (Privacy Policy & Terms of Service)

Both the App Store and Google Play require a URL for these documents.

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Simple path** | Create a dedicated "Privacy" (and optionally "Terms") page on your main deployment (e.g. voyagersanctuary.com/privacy, voyagersanctuary.com/terms). Publish the Privacy Policy text from [DATA_SAFETY_AND_PRIVACY.md](DATA_SAFETY_AND_PRIVACY.md) and this ToS. |
| **Fast path** | Use a GitHub Gist or a basic Notion page set to "Public," paste the Privacy Policy and ToS text, and paste the resulting link(s) into the store consoles. |

Use the same URL(s) in Google Play Console and App Store Connect where they ask for Privacy Policy (and Terms of Service if requested).
