# Where do I…?

Quick reference: **what you want to do** → **where to go in the app**.

---

## Adding things

| I want to… | Do this |
|------------|--------|
| **Add seeds from a link (URL)** | Tap **+** (FAB) → **Add Seed Packet** → choose **Link** / paste URL. |
| **Add seeds manually** | Tap **+** → **Add Seed Packet** → **Manual** (enter name, variety, vendor, etc.). |
| **Add seeds from a photo** | Tap **+** → **Add Seed Packet** → **Photo Import** (single or batch). |
| **Add seeds from an order screenshot** | Tap **+** → **Add Seed Packet** → **Purchase Order** (upload screenshot). |
| **Add a plant (tree, perennial, or seasonal)** | Tap **+** → **Add plant** → **Manual Entry** (or **Start Seeds** to plant from existing packets). |
| **Add supplies (fertilizer, soil, etc.)** | Tap **+** → **Add to shed** → then Manual, Photo, Link, or Purchase Order. |
| **Add a task** | Tap **+** → **Add task** (form opens on the current page). |
| **Log a quick note (with plants, date, photo)** | Tap **+** → **Add journal** (Quick Log modal). |
| **Add a full journal entry (more fields)** | Go to **Journal** → use **+** → **Add journal** for quick, or open **Full journal entry** from that modal; or go directly to `/journal/new`. |
| **Add an item to the shopping list** | From **Home**: Shopping list section → **Add item**. From **Shopping List** page: **+** → Add item. From a plant or supply: use **Add to Shopping List** on that item’s page. |

---

## Viewing and planning

| I want to… | Do this |
|------------|--------|
| **See when to plant (by zone)** | **Home** → **Planting Schedule** (Action now / Monthly pulse / Annual roadmap). Or **Settings** → **Resources** for zone charts. |
| **See my tasks (overdue, upcoming, done)** | Bottom nav → **Calendar**. |
| **See my seeds and packets** | Bottom nav → **Vault** (Plant Profiles or Seed Vault tab). |
| **See my supplies** | Bottom nav → **Vault** → **Shed** tab. |
| **See active plantings and my plants** | Bottom nav → **Garden** (Active Garden / My Plants tabs). |
| **See all journal entries** | Bottom nav → **Journal**. |
| **See my shopping list** | Header **cart icon** or **Home** → Shopping list section → **View full list**. |

---

## Editing and managing

| I want to… | Do this |
|------------|--------|
| **Edit a plant/variety (details, care, packets)** | **Vault** → tap the plant or packet → edit on the profile page. |
| **Edit a supply** | **Vault** → **Shed** tab → tap the supply. |
| **Edit a journal entry** | **Journal** → tap the entry (Edit Journal modal). |
| **Edit a task** | **Calendar** → tap the task (if supported) or complete it. |

---

## Notes

- The **+** button (FAB) is the same on **Home**, **Vault**, **Garden**, **Journal**, and **Calendar** — it always opens the same **Add** menu.
- **Shed** (supplies) lives inside **Vault** as a tab — switch to the Shed tab to see or add supplies.
- **Planting Schedule** (when to plant by zone) is linked from **Home** and **Resources**; **Calendar** is for your tasks and to-dos.
