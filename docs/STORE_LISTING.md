## 2. Long Description

The full narrative for the store page.

```
Welcome to the Sanctuary.

Voyager Sanctuary is not a place for noise, notifications, or the cluttered lists of the digital world. It is a dedicated space for your most meaningful work—a digital retreat designed to turn your goals into a visual journey.

Guided by Elias, your steady companion at the Hearth, you will transform your projects into Mountains and your tasks into Pebbles.

The Journey:

The Map: Visualize your progress across a hand-drawn landscape. Watch as your peaks ascend and your path clears with every step forward.

The Hearth: Engage in the daily ritual of the burn. Select your stones, commit to your focus, and clear the weight of the day in the warmth of the fire.

The Whetstone: Sharpen your habits. Build consistency through steady, daily practice that strengthens your resolve over time.

The Satchel: Carry only what you need. Organize your day by packing the stones that matter most, leaving the rest for another climb.

A Quiet Approach:

Total Stillness: An experience designed for focus, respecting your need for silence and concentration.

No Clutter: No social feeds, no ads, and no unnecessary complexity. Just you, your path, and the stones you choose to carry.

Elias: A guide who remembers your name and honors your progress, offering steady encouragement without the pressure of traditional productivity tools.

Set your intent. Start your climb. The Sanctuary is waiting.
```
