Voyager Sanctuary — Privacy Policy

Your path is private.

At Voyager Sanctuary, we believe your goals and reflections are yours alone. We do not sell, trade, or monitor your personal data for advertising.

What we collect

  • Identity: Your email address is used solely for authentication and account recovery (via Supabase Auth).
  • Your stones: We store the titles of your Mountains and Pebbles (and related content you create) so you can access them across devices. This data is protected by Row Level Security (RLS), meaning only you can access the content associated with your unique account.
  • Product interaction: Actions in the app (for example completing a task or using the Whetstone) may be stored to power streaks, momentum, and core features.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use advertising analytics SDKs in the app to track you across other companies’ apps or sites.

Supabase provides our database and authentication infrastructure; it is not a data broker for advertising.

Your control

  • Total deletion: You can delete your account and associated records from within the app (Settings). Deletion applies to data we store for your account.
  • Security: Data in transit is protected with HTTPS/TLS. Data at rest is handled by our infrastructure provider using industry-standard practices.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact support@voyagersanctuary.com.
